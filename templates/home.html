Introduction to Electric Fields

"Electric fields are a fundamental concept in physics that describe the influence a charged object has on other charged objects in its vicinity. Understanding electric fields is crucial for comprehending various phenomena in electromagnetism, electronics, and even everyday life."

How the Simulator Works

This electric field simulator allows you to visualize and interact with electric fields generated by various charge configurations. First enter number of desired charges, after that add the (x,y) coordinates and magnitude for each charge then press "Generate Field".
